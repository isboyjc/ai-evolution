# 人工智能75年 - AI Evolution

《人工智能75年》是一部记录人工智能发展历程的电子书，深度解析了从1950年图灵测试提出到现代AI技术生态的完整演进过程。本书约11万字，7个核心章节，系统性地呈现了人工智能领域每一次重大技术突破背后的逻辑和脉络。

https://ai-evolution.isboyjc.com

### 🎯 写作初衷

在AI技术快速发展的今天，很多朋友对AI充满兴趣，但对其了解往往停留在表面。无论是恐慌式学习还是真正想要深入了解AI，大多数人都不知道从哪里开始。

本书旨在为读者提供一个清晰的AI发展全景图，让每个人都能理解人工智能这场人类智慧大冒险的完整故事。

### 🌟 主要特色

- **系统性**：从技术起源到现代应用的完整脉络梳理
- **专业性**：基于大量国内外论文、文献的深度研究
- **可读性**：用通俗易懂的语言解释复杂的技术概念
- **时效性**：电子书形式支持持续更新和内容补充
- **交互性**：基于现代Web技术，提供优秀的阅读体验

# AI Evolution - 75 Years of Artificial Intelligence

*AI Evolution - 75 Years of Artificial Intelligence* is an e-book that chronicles the development of artificial intelligence, providing an in-depth analysis of the complete evolution from the Turing Test proposed in 1950 to the modern AI technology ecosystem. This book contains approximately 110,000 words across 7 core chapters, systematically presenting the logic and context behind every major technological breakthrough in the field of artificial intelligence.

https://ai-evolution.isboyjc.com

### 🎯 Why This Book

In today's era of rapid AI development, many people are fascinated by AI but their understanding often remains superficial. Whether driven by panic-induced learning or genuine desire to understand AI, most people don't know where to start.

This book aims to provide readers with a clear panoramic view of AI development, enabling everyone to understand the complete story of this great human intelligence adventure.

### 🌟 Key Features

- **Systematic**: Complete tracing from technological origins to modern applications
- **Professional**: In-depth research based on extensive domestic and international papers and literature
- **Accessible**: Complex technical concepts explained in easy-to-understand language
- **Timely**: E-book format supports continuous updates and content supplementation
- **Interactive**: Excellent reading experience based on modern web technology

## 📚 Chapters Overview

| Chapter | Title | Core Content |
|---------|-------|--------------|
| **Chapter 1** | [Origins of Intelligence](./content/book/01) | Exploring the origins of intelligence, the Turing Test established the first clear goal for AI's long journey |
| **Chapter 2** | [Rise and Fall of Symbolism](./content/book/02) | The complete process of AI's first dominant paradigm from birth, glory to eventual decline |
| **Chapter 3** | [Rise of Machine Learning](./content/book/03) | Connectionism, behaviorism, and statistical learning driving AI into the data-driven era |
| **Chapter 4** | [Deep Learning Breakthrough](./content/book/04) | Revolutionary breakthroughs achieved through automatic feature learning in deep learning |
| **Chapter 5** | [The Transformer Era](./content/book/05) | Transformer architecture ushering in the new era of large-scale pre-trained models |
| **Chapter 6** | [Taming Large Models](./content/book/06) | How to make powerful language models useful, reliable, and safe |
| **Chapter 7** | [Modern AI Technology Ecosystem](./content/book/07) | Modern AI application ecosystem built with RAG, Function Calling, Agent and other technologies |

## 🎯 Target Readers

This book is suitable for all readers interested in artificial intelligence:

- **Students**: Systematic understanding of AI development history and core concepts
- **Engineers**: Deep understanding of principles and applications of various AI technologies
- **Entrepreneurs**: Grasping AI technology development trends and business opportunities
- **General Readers**: Understanding AI's impact on society from a technical perspective
